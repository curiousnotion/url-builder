What does parameter tracking do?
  • Attributes lead generation and other user activities to campaigns within Google Analytics, Omniture & internal reporting systems.
  • Cues 3rd party ad network pixels to fire
  • Applies coupons to purchases
  • Allows marketing to create highly segmented re-marketing lists

The name of the Medium. Think of "Medium" as way you're getting our message in front a customer.

The name of the Ad Network. Think of "Medium" as the network that you're using to get in front of a customer in a given medium.

The audience that you're trying to reach. Also, shorthand for what you might search by in Google Analytics Report.

Examples are:

  • Females, Age:25-44, who have children ages 3-5, live in the US or Canada --> utm_campaingn=f-25-44-c-3-5-us-can
  • Males, Age:34-55, who are teachers, live in the US or Canada --> utm_campaingn=m-34-55-teach-us-can

Extra slot to input another dimension. Often used for the promotion being run, or the ad copy

Examples are:

  • Bainzy Orange Cat Ad for 15% Off --> utm_content=brain-o-v-15
  • PLUS Blue Dog Ad for 15% Off --> utm_content=plus-b-d-15

The value that you want set for your cid.
